    What are cookies?

    Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our articles (like "Azure & Gold Sessions" or seasonal planting guides), and improve your browsing experience. Think of them as little seed packets that help the site grow better for you.

    Managing cookies in your browser

    Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through settings (usually under "Privacy & Security"). However, disabling essential cookies may affect site features like the contact form or interactive garden planners. For any questions, feel free to email us at privacy@verdanthues.com.
